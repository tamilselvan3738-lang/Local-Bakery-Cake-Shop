Exhibition Centerpiece

The Golden Sovereign Cake

The Golden Sovereign Cake represents the pinnacle of our design studio's capability. Standing at four tiers tall, this masterpiece is coated in premium white fondant and hand-detailed with polished gold filigree patterns. Sweeping sheets of 24k edible gold leaf drape down the side, creating a majestic look.

Featured in numerous design magazines, it can be ordered exclusively through private consultations for high-end galas and grand weddings.

Inside Our Decorating Studio

Baking is science, but decorating is pure art. Inside our temperature-controlled design studio, our decorators work with precision instruments to sculpt fondant, pipe delicate royal icing details, and brush gold dust onto tiers.

We source organic edible colors, fresh garden rose petals, and premium gold leaf, guaranteeing that every cake is as safe and delicious to eat as it is stunning to look at.

Artisanal Cake Cutting Guide

Maximize your servings and keep slices neat with our professional round and square cake cutting guides.

Round Cake Grid Method

Instead of traditional wedges, cut round cakes in concentric rings 2 inches apart, then slice across to form neat, uniform portions.

Square Grid Method

Slice the square cake horizontally in 2-inch columns, then cut across vertically to divide them into perfect rectangular blocks.
