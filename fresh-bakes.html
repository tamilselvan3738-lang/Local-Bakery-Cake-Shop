Traditional Italian Steam-Injection Ovens

The secret behind our perfect sourdough crust lies in our Italian stone-deck ovens. By injecting clean steam at the initial phase of baking, we prevent the outer crust from setting too quickly, allowing the dough to expand fully and caramelize beautifully.

This creates a crisp shell, a glossy look, and lock in the interior moisture, which is why our breads stay fresh and delicious for days.
