Temperature-Controlled Delivery

Luxury tiered cakes are heavy and delicate. A slight temperature increase can melt buttercream roses or offset sugar pillars.

To guarantee your cake arrives in pristine museum-level condition, we deliver via our custom fleet of temperature-controlled vans. Our on-site catering team will assemble, place, and groom the tiers on your display table before guests arrive.
