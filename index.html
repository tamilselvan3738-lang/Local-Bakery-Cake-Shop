The Baker's Art

Stone-Milled & Slow-Baked

True baking is a slow craft. We stone-mill organic flour in-house to protect the essential fibers and vitamins. Our master boulangers maintain a 10-year-old natural wild sourdough starter, giving our breads their iconic open crumb structure and caramelized crust.

Every single croissant is hand-laminated with grass-fed churned butter, and baked in traditional German deck ovens utilizing steam injection to create flaky golden layers.
