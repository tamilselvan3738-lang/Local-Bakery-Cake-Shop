Cozy Tea Room

Step Inside Our French-Inspired Boutique

Our Paris-styled bakery boutique offers a warm, cozy retreat for sweet tooths. Designed with marble counters, gold-rimmed displays, and comfortable seating, it is the perfect spot to enjoy freshly baked croissants, signature desserts, and gourmet espresso.

We bake in small batches throughout the day, filling the air with the rich, buttery aroma of fresh deck-oven creations. Come find your new favorite sweet spot.

Master Pastry Chefs

Meet Our Passionate Baking Team

At Luxe Bakes, our master chefs turn baking into an art form. Combining years of classical French pastry training with creative modern design, our team handles everything from daily bread loaves to magnificent multi-tiered wedding cakes with delicate hand-sculpted frosting.

We pride ourselves on execution, using detailed piping, custom gold details, and organic ingredients to bake joy into every single slice.

The Fresh Bake Philosophy

We believe in the power of pure ingredients. That's why we source organic flour, locally churned butter, and fresh seasonal fruits for our bakes. No artificial preservatives, just classical sourdough fermentation and slow-rolled pastry layers.

Custom Celebration Cakes

Schedule a personal wedding cake tasting and consultation. We work closely with you to design a show-stopping multi-tiered cake complete with sugar floral details, gold leaf detailing, and custom flavor pairings.

Afternoon Tea Room

Book a table in our chic cafe seating area. Treat yourself to our weekend Afternoon Tea experience, featuring a tiered display of sandwiches, scones, macarons, and warm seasonal pastries paired with fine loose-leaf teas.
