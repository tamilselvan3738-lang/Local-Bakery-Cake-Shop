Our Bakery Heritage

A family-owned boutique confectionery baking love, tradition, and organic goodness daily since 1912.

Our Humble Beginnings

A Century of Sweet Traditions

"We do not just bake cakes; we bake memories that bind families across generations."

Luxe Bakes was founded in 1912 by master baker Henri Laurent in a small, warm stone shop on the corner of the local market place. Equipped with only a wood-fired deck oven and his grandmother's recipe notebook, Henri committed himself to a simple rule: source local flour, use natural starters, and bake with absolute patience.

Today, four generations later, we continue to honor Henri's legacy. While our boutique has expanded to include a modern custom design studio, our bakers still start their shifts at 3:00 AM daily, hand-kneading every sourdough boule and decorating every tiered cake with museum-level care.

Pure & Organic Sourcing

Only Fresh Ingredients

We believe that a cake is only as good as the raw ingredients loaded into the mixing bowl. That is why we refuse to use commercial pre-mixes, high-fructose corn syrups, or artificial preservatives.

We source organic stone-milled wheat from local family mills, grass-fed churned butter, organic local honey, farm-fresh eggs, and vanilla beans imported directly from Madagascar. It is a difference you can taste in every single bite.
