Our Sourdough Fermentation Timeline

0 Hours

Starter Mix

Activating our natural wild yeast culture starter with pure stone-milled wheat flour.

8 Hours

Autolyse Rise

Slowly hydrating dough sheets to naturally relax gluten bonds without additives.

16 Hours

Cold Ferment

Resting dough tubs at 4°C to release rich, complex lactic acid flavor profiles.

24 Hours

Stone Bake

Baking on hot deck plates with steam injections for a crisp mahogany outer crust.
